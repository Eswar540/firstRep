WELCOME TO THE DICE GAME

Rules:

1. Each player throws both dice once per turn. You only score if you throw doubles.

2.Players score five points for doubles ones,twos,fours or fives. A double six scores twenty five points, but if you throw a double three your score goes back to zero.

3.Add your score as you play.The first player to get fifty points wins the game.
